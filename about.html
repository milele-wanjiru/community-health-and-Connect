Our Story

Founded in 1995 by Dr. Michael Roberts and a group of dedicated healthcare professionals, Community Health and Connect began as a small community clinic with a simple mission: to make quality healthcare accessible to everyone.

Over the past three decades, we have grown from a modest 20-bed facility to a comprehensive medical center serving over 50,000 patients annually. Our expansion has been guided by our founding principles of compassion, excellence, and community partnership.

Today, we continue to innovate while staying true to our roots, ensuring that every patient receives the personalized care and attention they deserve.
